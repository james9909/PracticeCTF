The program checks if you've won by determining if your input contains the winning choice as a substring.
All we need to do is input a string containing all possible choices as substrings.

$ nc saturn.picoctf.net 51420
...
Please make your selection (rock/paper/scissors):
rockpaperscissors
rockpaperscissors
You played: rockpaperscissors
The computer played: paper
You win! Play again?
Congrats, here's the flag!
